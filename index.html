A more spacious Crowd Control experience and direct access to the Global Coin store are just a click away.

Step 1: Install the Bookmarklet

Click and drag the button above onto your Bookmarks or Favorites bar so you can access it from anywhere

Step 2: Find a Stream

Settle in with your favorite interactive gamer, or maybe discover someone new

Step 3: Click on the Bookmarklet

Clicking the bookmarklet while you're on a Twitch stream provides a new Crowd Control button for easy access to the effects list, and the Global Coin store!
